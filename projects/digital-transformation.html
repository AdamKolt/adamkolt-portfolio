Digital Transformation

Telecom Sector B2B Self-Service Platform Lead Sales & Marketing Digital Transformation
Ooredoo Qatar is a leading telecom operator managing B2B and B2C services across 13 countries. The B2B segment faced mounting pressure to modernize its sales and service delivery model for enterprise and SMB customers.
Problem: The B2B sales process was entirely manual — account managers handled every order, renewal, and service change. This created bottlenecks, slow turnaround times, high cost-to-serve, and an inability to scale. Customers had no self-service capability.
Proposition: Design and deliver a B2B Digital Sales & Self-Service Platform—an end-to-end digital channel allowing business customers to browse, configure, purchase, and manage Ooredoo services online, while equipping the sales team with a modern CPQ and CRM-integrated sales portal.

Led the concept, design, build, and launch of Ooredoo Qatar's first B2B digital sales and self-service platform — transforming a fully manual sales operation into a scalable, digital-first channel.

Strategic Vision & Business Case: Defined the digital transformation roadmap for the B2B segment. Built the business case including competitive benchmarking, customer research, revenue opportunity modeling, and a phased delivery plan. Secured C-suite sponsorship and budget approval.
Platform Architecture: Designed the end-to-end solution architecture spanning a customer-facing self-service portal, an agent-facing sales portal with CPQ capability, CRM integration (Salesforce), and a product catalog engine. Defined all integration APIs between front-end, middleware, and BSS/OSS back-end systems.
UX & Journey Design: Led low-fidelity wireframing of all key customer journeys including product discovery, quote generation, online checkout, contract signing (eSign), and self-service account management. Collaborated with UX/UI team to translate flows into high-fidelity prototypes validated through customer testing sessions.
Agile Delivery: Owned the product backlog and drove sprint planning across a cross-functional squad of 20+ members including developers, QA, UX designers, and integration engineers. Conducted daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives. Delivered the MVP in 6 months followed by iterative feature releases.
Ecosystem Integration: Coordinated integration with Salesforce CRM, Oracle BSS billing, product catalog, digital identity (SSO), e-signature, and payment gateway. Defined data contracts, API specifications, and end-to-end test scenarios across 8 integrated systems.

Sales Enablement & Training: Designed the sales enablement program to transition account managers from order-takers to value-based consultants. Created sales playbooks, product training, and digital tool adoption programs for 150+ sales staff.

Launch & Results: Delivered the platform on time and within budget. Within the first quarter, online self-service orders accounted for a significant share of new B2B activations, reducing average order processing time by over 70% and lowering cost-to-serve substantially.
