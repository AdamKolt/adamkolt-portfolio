Commercial Software

B2C Lean Integration & Operations Management COTS Methodology Commercial Off-the-Shelf
Saber Consulting was a U.S.-based technology firm specializing in Commercial Off-the-Shelf (COTS) software deployment for utility and insurance sectors. The firm grew from a regional operator to a nationally recognized integrator across 13 U.S. states.
Problem: The company lacked a scalable, repeatable delivery methodology for COTS system implementations. Each project was executed ad hoc, resulting in cost overruns, delayed timelines, and inconsistent client outcomes. The business needed a standardized approach to grow nationally.
Proposition: Design and implement a proprietary COTS Integration Methodology — a repeatable, lean-based framework for deploying commercial software across multi-state clients — enabling the company to scale rapidly, win larger contracts, and achieve acquisition-level growth.

The AAA Auto Club Group — one of the largest insurance and roadside-assistance organizations in the U.S. — selected Saber Consulting to deploy a new commercial policy management system across its member states. This engagement became the proving ground for a methodology that would transform the company.

Requirements Engineering: Led comprehensive requirements gathering workshops across AAA business units including Policy Administration, Claims, Billing, and Member Services. Produced detailed functional specifications, UML use-case diagrams, and BPMN process maps documenting both as-is and to-be states across 13 member states.
COTS Methodology Design: Developed a proprietary COTS implementation framework incorporating Lean principles, standardized configuration templates, data migration playbooks, and a phased go-live strategy. The methodology reduced average project setup time by 40% and was subsequently applied to all future client engagements.
Business Process Reengineering: Facilitated deep-dive process redesign workshops to align client workflows to COTS system capabilities, minimizing customization cost while maximizing system ROI. Delivered value stream maps and swimlane diagrams to visualize optimized end-to-end processes.
Data Migration Strategy: Architected the data migration approach including source-to-target mapping, data quality assessment, cleansing rules, and parallel-run validation. Managed migration of millions of policy and member records with zero critical data loss incidents.
Stakeholder & Vendor Management: Coordinated across AAA executive sponsors, IT teams, and the COTS software vendor to manage scope, resolve escalations, and ensure contractual obligations were met. Maintained a RACI-driven governance model with weekly steering committee reporting.
Multi-State Rollout: Managed concurrent deployments across multiple U.S. states, adapting the core implementation to state-specific regulatory and operational requirements. Coordinated local change management and user training programs for each state rollout.
