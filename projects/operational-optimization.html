Operational Optimization

Ooredoo Qatar NPS & CSAT Revenue Optimization Workforce Planning
Revamped the end-to-end B2B sales and service operation at Ooredoo Qatar, resulting in measurable revenue growth and significant improvements in both NPS (Net Promoter Score) and CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) metrics.
Deployed a suite of six interconnected operational initiatives — spanning sales analytics, workforce planning, process automation, customer experience, product performance, and executive reporting — each grounded in data and designed to compound over time.

The following six initiatives were executed concurrently, each targeting a distinct operational lever. Together they formed a unified transformation of the sales and service engine.

1

Sales Performance Dashboard

Designed and deployed a real-time sales analytics dashboard in Power BI, integrating data from Salesforce CRM, billing systems, and the product catalog. The dashboard gave sales managers live visibility into pipeline health, conversion rates, quota attainment, and revenue-at-risk — replacing weekly manual reports with instant insight. Enabled daily coaching conversations grounded in data rather than anecdote.

Sales Dashboard (Tablet)
2

Workforce Planning & Capacity Model

Built a dynamic workforce planning model in Excel/Power BI to align staffing levels with demand forecasts across retail, telesales, and field sales channels. The model incorporated seasonality, product launch calendars, attrition projections, and training ramp times — enabling HR and Sales leadership to make proactive hiring decisions 90 days ahead of demand peaks, reducing understaffing incidents and overtime costs.

Workforce Model (Excel)
3

Order & Provisioning Process Redesign

Conducted end-to-end value stream mapping of the B2B order-to-activation journey, identifying 14 process waste points including manual handoffs, redundant approvals, and data re-entry between systems. Redesigned the workflow and automated 6 manual steps through Salesforce Flow and integration with the OSS provisioning system, reducing average order-to-activation time from 9 days to 3 days.

Process Map (BPMN)
4

NPS & CSAT Improvement Program

Established Ooredoo Qatar's first structured Voice of the Customer (VoC) program for the B2B segment. Implemented post-interaction NPS surveys across all touchpoints (sales, provisioning, support). Built a closed-loop feedback mechanism requiring account managers to follow up on all detractors within 48 hours. Drove NPS improvement of +18 points over 12 months and lifted CSAT from 67% to 84%.

NPS Tracker (Mobile)
5

Product Portfolio Rationalization

Led a comprehensive product portfolio review using unit economics analysis — assessing margin contribution, attach rates, competitive positioning, and customer lifetime value for each product SKU. Identified 8 underperforming products for discontinuation and 3 high-potential products for accelerated investment. Reallocated sales incentives and marketing budget to highest-margin products, contributing to a 12% improvement in revenue per account.

Portfolio Analysis (Excel)
6

Executive Reporting & Revenue Intelligence

Architected a monthly Revenue Intelligence Pack for C-suite consumption — consolidating KPIs across revenue, churn, NPS, workforce utilization, and pipeline into a single, visually consistent deck. Built the underlying ETL pipelines (SQL + Power BI) to automate data extraction and refresh. Eliminated 40 hours/month of manual report preparation and gave leadership a single source of truth for strategic decision-making.
